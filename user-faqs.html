    Frequently Asked Questions

    EcoFinds is a platform where people can post their unused or unwanted items for others to claim for free. It's a way to reduce waste and help the community.

    Anyone with an account on the platform can post items they no longer need.

    You can post items that are still usable and materials or items that can be recycled. Avoid posting hazardous materials or broken items.

    When NGOs sign up and verify their organization, they are notified first when new items that match their needs are posted.

    If your item is not claimed within 3 months, we will send you a notification suggesting nearby donation centers or recycling facilities.

    NGOs can register on the platform to receive notifications about items they may need.
